Meet Divya Tripathi

With over 15+ years of Vedic astrology experience, our astrologer has guided thousands toward peace and clarity. His knowledge of planetary alignments, karma, and kundli decoding empowers individuals to shape their destiny.

Divya Tripathi

Swami Vipin Vayas is one of the most renowned and respected Adhyatmik (Spiritual) Darshanik (Philosophical) Gurus, Proficient Yogi, and Astrologer whose spiritual journey began over 40 years ago amidst the serene valleys of Amarnath, immersed in Sadhana and meditation. His path later led him to Kamakhya, the sacred abode of the ten Mahavidyas - the feminine divine.

From early childhood, Swami Vipin Vayas was surrounded by saints, sages, and seers. His formative years were deeply influenced by the sacred atmosphere of temples and monasteries, where devotional Kirtans, Bhajans, and holy rituals were part of his daily life.

His spiritual journey began in his youth under the divine guidance of his father. It was from his father that Swami ji first absorbed the sacred knowledge of astrology, observing with curiosity and reverence as his father calculated planetary dashas and timelines through intricate Kundali (birth charts).

While most children attended conventional schools, young Vipin embarked on divine pilgrimages. His teenage years were marked by deep meditative journeys through the sacred lands of Amarnath and the mystic Nimalchal Parvat, in search of the Supreme Truth that lies beyond the material world.

Swami ji was introduced to the practices of Sadhana, Siddhi, and Upasana, igniting a spiritual flame that would guide his life in understanding the Supreme Power of the cosmos.

Moving forward in the path of spirituality, he decided to permanently dedicate his spiritual knowledge for the well-being of others. Since then, he has devoted himself to guiding seekers on the path of self-realization, inner peace, and divine wisdom.

For over three decades, Swami Vipin Vayas has practiced occult and spiritual sciences, mastering the following disciplines:

  • Spiritual Astrology
  • Birth Journals (Kundli Analysis)
  • Numerology
  • Vedic and Tantric Geomancy (Vastu & Earth Energies)
  • Tarot Card Reading
  • Navagrah Shanti
  • Vivah Dosh Puja
  • Kaal Sarp Dosh Puja
  • Palmistry
  • Planetary Dosh Nivaran
  • Najar Dosh
  • Pitra Dosh
  • Prediction of Future with Face Reading
